The IT industry in Vietnam continues to maintain a higher salary range compared to many other industries, especially in Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi. These two cities are currently the country’s largest technology recruitment hubs, attracting outsourcing companies, product-based firms, fintech businesses, AI startups, and international tech corporations.

### Average IT Salary in Vietnam

According to data from Indeed Vietnam updated in 2026, the average salary in the IT industry is approximately:

*   Nationwide: around 20.8 million VND/month
*   Hanoi: around 22.9 million VND/month
*   Ho Chi Minh City: around 21.3 million VND/month

Sources:

*   Indeed Vietnam
*   Indeed Hanoi

### IT Salary by Experience Level

#### 1\. Intern / Fresher (0 - 1 year of experience)

This group mainly includes university graduates and interns. Salaries at this level depend heavily on practical skills, English proficiency, and technology stack expertise.

*   Intern: 3 - 7 million VND/month
*   Fresher Developer: 7 - 15 million VND/month
*   Freshers with strong English skills or product-company experience: 12 - 18 million VND/month

Common positions:

*   Frontend Developer
*   Backend Developer
*   QA/Tester
*   IT Support

Source:

*   FPT BTEC summary based on TopDev, ITviec, and TopCV

#### 2\. Junior Developer (1 - 3 years of experience)

This is currently the most common level in the market. Developers at this stage are usually capable of working independently and participating in real-world projects.

*   Frontend/Backend Developer: 15 - 30 million VND/month
*   Tester/QA: 15 - 25 million VND/month
*   Mobile Developer: 18 - 35 million VND/month

In Ho Chi Minh City, salaries are often higher for product companies and fintech businesses. Meanwhile, Hanoi has a strong presence of Japanese outsourcing and enterprise projects.

Source:

*   TopCV Salary Report 2025 summarized by Viecoi

#### 3\. Middle Developer (3 - 5 years of experience)

This is currently the most in-demand group in the market. Developers at this level often begin leading modules or mentoring junior team members.

*   Mid-level Backend/Frontend Developer: 30 - 45 million VND/month
*   DevOps Engineer: 35 - 55 million VND/month
*   Data Engineer / AI Engineer: 40 - 70 million VND/month

Foreign product companies and well-funded startups in Ho Chi Minh City may offer significantly higher salaries.

Sources:

*   JobForge Salary Report 2025 - 2026
*   TopCV Salary Report 2025

#### 4\. Senior Developer (5+ years of experience)

Senior developers are among the most sought-after professionals after the market recovery period. Engineers with architecture, leadership, or cloud expertise often receive exceptionally high salaries.

*   Senior Developer: 45 - 70 million VND/month
*   Tech Lead: 60 - 90 million VND/month
*   Engineering Manager: 80 - 120 million VND/month

Highest-paying areas at the moment:

*   AI/ML Engineering
*   Cloud/DevOps
*   Cyber Security
*   Data Engineering
*   Blockchain

Sources:

*   TopDev
*   Levels.fyi Vietnam IT Salary

### Ho Chi Minh City vs Hanoi

#### Ho Chi Minh City

*   Home to many international product companies and startups
*   Higher salary ranges in frontend, mobile, and fintech sectors
*   More opportunities for international remote jobs
*   Faster salary growth and job-hopping culture

#### Hanoi

*   Strong in enterprise, banking, and Japanese outsourcing projects
*   Many government, telecom, and large fintech projects
*   High demand for DevOps, Java, and System Engineers
*   More stable market but slower salary growth compared to Ho Chi Minh City

### IT Salary Trends in 2026

#### AI and Data Continue to Lead

According to multiple recruitment reports, AI Engineers and Data Engineers are currently experiencing the fastest salary growth. Experienced professionals in these fields can earn between 70 - 120 million VND/month.

#### English Becomes a Mandatory Skill

The salary gap between developers with strong English skills and those without can range from 20% to 50%.

#### International Remote Work is Growing Rapidly

Many developers in Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi are now working remotely for companies in Singapore, Japan, and the United States with salaries significantly higher than the domestic average.

### Conclusion

The IT salary market in Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi remains among the highest-paying sectors in Vietnam. However, the income gap between experience levels is becoming increasingly large.

*   Fresher: 7 - 15 million VND
*   Junior: 15 - 30 million VND
*   Middle: 30 - 45 million VND
*   Senior: 45 - 90+ million VND

To accelerate income growth in the IT industry today, the most valuable career paths include:

*   Cloud/DevOps
*   AI/Data
*   Cyber Security
*   Product Engineering
*   Strong English communication skills
