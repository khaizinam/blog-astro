Welcome to the about page of **KhaiziNam Blog**!

I am a software engineer based in Vietnam, specializing in Web Development (Backend & DevOps).

This blog was created as a personal space to document and share technology knowledge, real-world development experiences, and lessons learned from coding and debugging.

## Topics I focus on sharing:
- **Backend Development**: PHP, Laravel, Node.js (Express, NestJS).
- **Database & Caching**: MySQL, PostgreSQL, Redis, and query optimization techniques.
- **DevOps & Infrastructure**: Docker, Docker Compose, Linux, Nginx, CI/CD, and real-world application deployment.
- **Personal Development**: Productivity, system administration, and debugging tips.

## The Goal of This Blog
In addition to sharing technical guides, this blog is designed to support **Bilingual Content (Vietnamese - English)** to reach a wider audience.

I believe that the best way to master a new technology is to **explain it to others as simply as possible**. I hope the articles here help you on your coding journey!
